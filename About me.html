who
i am

Excellence is not a skill, it's an attitude. – Ralph Marston
Success is the result of perfection, hard work, learning from failure, loyalty, and persistence. – Colin Powell

These two quotes perfectly capture my perception of success and excellence. Success is not something that happens by chance, nor does it fall from the sky—it's the consequence of consistent effort and dedication. Taking action is key. Most people already know what they need to do to achieve their goals, but many lack the motivation to act. My mentality is simple: always strive to do my best and be the best in my field. I put in the effort and the resources necessary to succeed. Even though I don’t know who will read this, welcome to my mind.

I’m a big dreamer. I still believe in the childlike wonder that makes me think anything is possible. I believe that big dreams shape the world—innovative and groundbreaking ideas come from those who dare to dream and believe in themselves. Along my journey, I’ve encountered many failures and disappointments, but those setbacks didn’t break the dreamer in me. In fact, it’s those very failures that make success even sweeter. If you want something, go after it. I’m a fighter, a wrestler, and I put in the work. I love collaborating with people because it allows me to learn and grow. I believe in my own excellence, but I also recognize and respect the excellence in others. Humility is important—being open to advice, regardless of someone’s age or background, is vital to continuous growth.
